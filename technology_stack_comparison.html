🎯 Personalized Recommendations for Ferrari Driving School
🚀 Quick Win (Next 2 weeks)

Start with No-Code approach using Zapier to optimize your current OpenAI setup. Add basic caching for FAQ responses and implement Claude 3 Haiku for 70% cost reduction. This gives you immediate benefits while you plan the next phase.

⚖️ Strategic Choice (Months 2-4)

Migrate to Custom Development using Node.js + LangGraph. This gives you the performance and cost benefits you need for long-term success. Your 3,000 messages/month volume justifies the development investment.

🔮 Future Scaling (Year 2+)

As you expand to multiple channels and grow to 10,000+ messages/month, your custom solution will pay for itself. Consider Enterprise features only when you have 100+ students active monthly.
